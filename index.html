Dr. Glenski leads the Foundational Data Science group in the AI and Data Analytics division of Pacific Northwest National Laboratory's National Security Directorate. Prior to leading the Foundational Data Science group, she was the Computational Social Science Team Lead. Her research focuses on modeling, characterizing, and explaining complex behavior of humans, AI/ML, and human-AI collaborative systems. Examples include:

  • disinformation spread and engagement in diverse online social environments,
  • social response to COVID-19 non-pharmaceutical interventions,
  • trusted and responsible AI evaluation,
  • scalable AI (LLMs, foundation models) for science and security.
