About Me

I am the Computational Social Science Team Lead in the Foundational Data Science group (Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics division). My research focuses on developing and applying novel methods for trusted and responsible AI evaluation, explainable AI, and computational social science approaches to explain, predict, and prescribe complex systems and behaviors (e.g., disinformation, social response to COVID-19 non-pharmaceutical interventions). The most up to date list of my publications is available on my Google Scholar profile.
